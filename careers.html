  • BI Engineer

    We are looking for a Full-time BI Engineer to join our team in Kathmandu.

    As a BI Engineer at Oval Analytics, you will use data to inform business and product decisions across to our clients in Nepal and abroad. You will work with our data engineers and build tools to automate reporting and generate insight that will allow clients to quickly and accurately see how their business processes are performing. You’ll be involved in projects from inception to delivery, ensuring that your reporting delivers high-quality and relevant insights.

    Key Responsibilities
    • Work closely with our clients to understand their information needs, ensuring that the BI strategy and agreed solutions are an excellent fit to their evolving needs.
    • Manage, own and deliver multiple BI work-streams, both on an ongoing and ad-hoc basis.
    • Work with the rest of the team to help shape and drive the development of our BI infrastructure including Data Warehousing, reporting and analytics platforms.
    • Contribute to the development of the BI team’s skills and culture.
    • Participate in strategic and tactical planning discussions.

    Must Have
    • Strong database experience including ability to write basic SQL queries.
    • Knowledge of commercial and emerging reporting tools and technologies (e.g. Excel, SPSS, Acess, R, SAS, Tableau).
    • Bachelors degree in Computer Science/Engineering with strong mathematics skills
    • Proven experience developing and delivering Business Intelligence applications.
    • Genuine excitement and passion for developing and analyzing complex data sets and converting them into the information / insights that drive business decisions at all levels of the organization.

    Good to Have
    • Demonstrated ability to frame complex analytical problems in simple terms, pull data, and extract insights that led to tangible results.
    • Adept in using a variety of tools to solve the problem at hand, and always ready to learn new ones as the need arises.
    • Experience working in an analytical data warehouses environment and ETL tools.
    • Outstanding speaking, writing, and presentation skills, as well as the ability to persuade, inspire, and motivate others. Summarize key insights for technical and non-technical audiences.
    • Experience communicating with senior management as well as with colleagues from computer science and business backgrounds.
    • MBA or Masters degree in an analytic field.
    • Can separate the data signal from the noise.

    We offer good compensation based on experience along with other perks that come with joining a startup.
