Who We Are

Our Story

The foundations of Oval Analytics were laid in late 2013 after the serendipitous meeting of Hemanta Shrestha, a seasoned analytics professional from the US and Saurav Dhungana, a telecom engineer turned Data Scientist living in Europe. Both of them returned to their home country Nepal to explore opportunities here, as they understood the tremendous opportunities that lie in emerging field of Data Science and Big Data. Soon after that they joined forces and started Oval Analytics in Kathmandu on the basis of their complementary skills and unified vision. Oval also has a regional office in San Diego, California, USA.

Oval is the first of its kind tech company in Nepal, and aims to introduce and provide data-driven analytics services in Nepal and also other markets around the world. This is a challenge, but one the team has taken up with full enthusiasm. Great things lie ahead in Data Science and Oval Analytics is set to become an important part of it.

Leadership

Hemanta Shrestha, Ph.D.
Co-Founder & CEO

Hemanta is the CEO of Oval Analytics and holds a Ph.D. in Economics from the University of Connecticut, USA. He has more than 14 years of experience in Corporate America, where he primarily worked in the Telecom sector for companies like AT&T, Sprint, Nokia and Cox Communications.

Hemanta is very passionate about the application of Data Science in business. He believes that every business should make “smart decisions” using data-driven analytics to stay ahead in today's competitive global marketplace.

Saurav Dhungana
Co-Founder & CTO

Saurav is a polyglot engineer who got his Masters in Communications Engineering from Aalto University, Finland. He has helped large Telecoms understand their customers' web usage behaviour, as well as worked with companies like Google to study the effectiveness of mobile ads.

As the CTO of Oval Analytics, Saurav is responsible for the overall vision and development strategy of its product and solutions. A proponent of design thinking he aims to incorporate its processes into the work culture of the company and create great things.
