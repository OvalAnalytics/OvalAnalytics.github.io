Who We Are

Our Story

Oval is the first of its kind tech company in Nepal, and aims to introduce and provide data-driven analytics services in Nepal and also other markets around the world. This is a challenge, but one the team has taken up with full enthusiasm. Great things lie ahead in Data Science and Oval Analytics is set to become an important part of it.Oval has offices in San Diego, California, USA and Kathmandu, Nepal.

Leadership

Hemanta Shrestha, Ph.D.
Co-Founder & CEO

Hemanta is the CEO of Oval Analytics and holds a Ph.D. in Economics from the University of Connecticut, USA. He has more than 14 years of experience in Corporate America, where he worked for companies like AT&T, Sprint, Nokia and Cox Communications.

Saurav Dhungana
Co-Founder & CTO

Saurav is a polyglot engineer who got his Masters in Communications Engineering from Aalto University, Finland. He has helped large Telecoms understand their customers' web usage behaviour, as well as worked with companies like Google to study the effectiveness of mobile ads.

Mark Donnelly
COO & Head of US Operations

Mark has over a decade of hands on experience providing financial and operational analytics for companies such as American Express and Sony. His strength lie in applying rigorous analysis, in a range of business functions to provide tools for senior executives.
