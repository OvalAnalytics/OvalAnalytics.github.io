Oval Summer Internship Program (OSIP)




Oval Analytics believes analytics is critical to deeply connect with customers, earn customer loyalty, make better decisions, and ultimately improve the whole customer experience for any business. Companies from different verticals partner with Oval to build breakthrough analytic solutions, set up analytical centers of excellence, and create a culture of data driven decision making. We solve problems, operationalize solutions to drive results, and ultimately drive change in organizations towards fact-based decision making. We help businesses:

  1. Understand, predict and influence consumer behavior.
  2. Improve marketing, pricing, supply chain, risk and claims management.
  3. Harmonize data, visualize information, build dashboards and forecast business performance.


The Internship

Oval Analytics is invested in developing the expertise of individuals in Data Science – and particularly, fostering Oval Analytics’ commitment to excellence, innovation and enhancing collegiality among researchers new to the profession. The integral part of these efforts is the Oval Summer Internship Program (OSIP), which offers two 4-week, paid positions for students pursuing undergraduate and graduate-level studies in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics and Business Managment. Interns are typically assigned to an ongoing Oval Analytics research projects, attend a seminar series on principles of survey research, and conduct a small-scale study as an intern group project. Each summer, interns will:

  • Learn the application of analytical tools and gain hands-on experience with real life experience in the business world.
  • Apply and develop technical skills you have learned in schools and colleges.

The Opportunity
  • You’ll help us solve real problems.. You won’t just experience the thrill of solving interesting problems; you’ll have the satisfaction of knowing your work makes a difference. We help our clients grow, but we also devote significant resources to projects that do good.
  • We’ll take you seriously. Are you an engineer with an interest in machine learning? Or a business major who wants a glimpse of how analytics is being used in business? Your work as an intern will be valued as if you were an employee.
  • We’re still small. Because we're a startup, you will get full leeway to choose what you want to do.
  • Does this feel like home? We view internships as extended job interviews. There are opportunities to come back full time.

What you will be doing

The interns will learn about our work and how our technology helps our analytics capability. You can choose among the following disciplines.

  1. Software Engineering

    Help build tools that will help automate the analytics and data visualization process. Interns will learn about our work and how our technology helps our analytics capability.

  2. Busineess Intelligence

    Work on research projects to diagnose client needs, help develop analytical strategies, and execute on client-focused work. You will work directly on client needs and prepare client-focused materials.

  3. Data Research
    We believe you can’t solve big problems without the right data. Work with our Data Team to research, standardize, and transform a wide variety of data sets to prepare them for analysis and build statistical models.

Are you the right fit?

Based in Oval Analytics’ Headquarter in Bhatbhateni, the interns will be assigned to particular project at Oval and will work with our team to help translate the analysis into meaningful impact to the business. Please note that candidates will have a general interview and be assigned tasks.

Each intern will develop skills and abilities to manage programs and engage the analytics and business solution projects, case studies etc. Candidates should have a desire to pursue a business, mathematics, statistics and computer science career with an interest in business world and entrepreneurship.

Application Process

This internship is open for students currently enrolled in bachelor or masters level studies or those who are fresh graduates, with less than 1 year of work experience. Students from both Nepal and abroad may apply. Interested applicants should submit a recent resume and an application letter that outlines your interest and qualifications for the internship with Oval Analytics. Please clearly specify the position you are applying for and send your application to the attention of Oval Analytics no later than June 18, 2014.

Applications may be sent electronically to: interns@ovalanalytics.com. Applications received after the deadline will not be considered.
